About Imbeko

Our Story

Pioneering Travel Excellence Since 2016

Imbeko Travel & Hospitality (PTY) LTD was founded on 03 March 2016 with a vision to transform the travel experience for businesses and individuals across South Africa. What started as a small agency in East London has grown into a trusted partner for corporate, government, and leisure travelers nationwide.

As a proudly Black-owned business, we are committed to economic transformation while delivering world-class travel services. Our IATA and ASATA accreditations reflect our dedication to industry standards and excellence.

Today, we serve clients across multiple sectors, including government departments, municipalities, parastatals, and corporate enterprises seeking premium travel experiences.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional travel and hospitality solutions that exceed client expectations, while contributing to economic transformation and sustainable tourism in Africa. We aim to be a Black Empowered Company with a reputation for Service Excellence, Transparency, Efficiency, Total Quality Management, flexibility, Reliability and cost-effective Pricing.

Our Vision

To be an International leader and South African based Travel and Hospitality management Company. To be dynamic, innovative and progressive in all our service offerings, providing National competitiveness and pioneering Travel and Hospitality Services.
