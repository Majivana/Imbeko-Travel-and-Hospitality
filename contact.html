Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I make a booking?

You can make a booking by contacting us via phone (043 050 5142), email (info@imbeko.co.za), or through this contact form. Our team will assist you with finding the best options for your travel needs.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, EFT, and corporate account payments. For government clients, we work with official purchase orders and NT/CSD compliance.

Can I make changes to my booking?

Yes, most bookings can be modified. Change fees may apply depending on the airline or hotel policy. Contact us as soon as possible to make changes - our 24/7 emergency line is available at 072 8296 138.

Do you offer corporate accounts?

Yes, we offer comprehensive corporate travel management solutions with dedicated account managers, customized reporting, and preferential rates. Contact us for a corporate quote.
